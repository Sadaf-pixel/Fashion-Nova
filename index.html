Fashion designing is more than a profession, it's art. To work with art, you need to know how to balance aesthetics that bring out the natural beauty of a person. Haute Couture


Haute-couture in fashion serves high-end and unique clothing designs. It is custom-made clothing for individual clients.

Luxury Fashion


Luxury fashion also serves a high-end clientele, its high-quality garments aren't as restricted.

Economy Fashion


In this type of design, garments are mass-produced efficiently and inexpensively.

Fast Fashion


Fast fashion follows high-end designing styles only the designs are tweaked and manufactured for a larger audience.
